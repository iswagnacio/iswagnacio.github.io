Project 1

Images of the Russian Empire: Colorizing the Prokudin-Gorskii photo collection

Project Overview

This project utilizes Sobel edge detection, Normalized Cross-Correlation, and image pyramid to colorize the digitized glass plate negatives from Sergei Mikhailovich Prokudin-Gorskii's collection of early 20th century Russian Empire photographs. Each glass plate contains three separate exposures of the same scene taken through red, green, and blue filters. The goal is to extract these three channels and align them to produce a single color RGB image with minimal visual artifacts.

Bells and whistles: Use Sobel Edge detection to extract structural features
